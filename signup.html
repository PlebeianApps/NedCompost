Why Compost?

  • By signing up you are contributing to Zero-Waste in Nederland. Composting your food scraps and organic waste builds valuable food growing capacity for your hometown, reduces landfill methane gasses, and supports a local business. Get you first 2 weeks free when you sign up for service online.
