About Nederland Composting@NedCompost

NedCompost has been in operation for a little over a year and is devoted to bringing zero-waste to the mountains of Colorado. NedCompost is a non-profit working on 100% human and renewable power and is therefore 100% reliable. Compost generates wealth by building rich soil for area.
